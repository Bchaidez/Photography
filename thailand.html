THAILAND

These photographs were taken during my study abroad trip in 2013. I studied abroad in Chiang Mai, Thailand with an NGO called Loop Abroad. During my trip I focused on indigenous tribes, education, and conservation. The beginning of my summer was spent living with a hill tribe community. There I learned and was involved with the local system. The second part of my trip was spent at The Elephant Nature Park learning about the abuse many elephants face and rehabilitating the rescued elephants at the park.
